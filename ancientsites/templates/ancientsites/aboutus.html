Hi

Basically I am an architect from Sir JJ College of Architecture, Mumbai (1989 batch) and has practiced architecture for almost 10 years from 1989-2001.

I am also an ardent trekker and have trekked/climbed 230 forts of Maharashtra along with Sanjay Jambhulkar my life-Partner. I am a Vipassana meditator since 1989 and has undertaken short and long courses (12 days -30 days)under the Goenka tradition.

My first PhD (2005) is on the topic “Impact of Vipassana Meditation on Buddhist Architecture” from Mumbai University. In this research I have practically visited and studied almost all the architectural and archaeological sites of India and have meditated there noting down my experience of meditation.

My second PhD (2011) is in the language Pali on the topic “Mnemonics in Pali Literature” from Mumbai University where I have tried to show the different memorization techniques taught by the Buddha.

I became a teacher of Pali at the University of Mumbai in 2005 when the MA Pali course was introduced. I am Coordinator of the Department of Pali since it came intoexistence in June 2007.

I have studied Buddhism while doing my Masters in Philosophy, Masters in Pali, Masters in Ancient IndianCulture and Masters in Numismatics and archaeology.

I am a research guide for Pali and am guiding more than15 students for MPhil in Pali (5 MPhil awarded) and 03 students are pursuing PhD under my guidance now. The Department of Pali is a place in the University of Mumbai which has more than 300 students learning Pali in 10 different courses. The students are not only trained in the language but are introduced to all aspects of Buddhist art, architecture, archaeology and culture through various activities conducted at the Department.
